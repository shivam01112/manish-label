Leathers We Produce

Eco-Treated Leather Label

Experience the rich and distinct aesthetic of our leather labels, crafted using eco-conscious tanning processes. Specifically treated to withstand home-washing, these labels are designed to minimize shrinkage and prevent spotting or bleeding, ensuring longevity and consistent appearance even with frequent use.

Chromium Free Leather

While Chromium tanning has been the dominant process in the market due to its short production process, well-settled colour and production cost, its environmental and health drawbacks cannot be overlooked. At Ideax, we prioritize both quality and responsibility. We opt for sustainable alternatives, embracing natural dyeing methods like vegetable tanning to produce leather that’s not only high-quality but also eco-friendly and safe for artisans.

Compact Leather

Maximizing the value of every leather piece, compact leather gives new life to imperfect materials otherwise discarded. Through a minimal chemical tanning process and the addition of artificial coating, we produce leather labels indistinguishable from regular ones. This method ensures reduced environmental impact and enhanced cost efficiency, backed by our OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification.

Bonded Leather

Our approach to bonded leather epitomizes sustainable innovation. We meticulously shred and bond leather remnants, transforming them into unique and stylish products. This technique not only minimizes waste and reduces landfill contributions but also ensures a high cutting yield, making it a cost-effective solution. Proudly GRS Certified.
